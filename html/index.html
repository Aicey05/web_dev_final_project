Detailed Service Information

Individual Counseling

$120 per session

Individual counseling provides a safe and confidential space for you to explore your thoughts, feelings, and behaviors with a trained professional. Our counselors use evidence-based approaches to help you address challenges, develop coping skills, and work toward your personal goals.

What to Expect

  • Personalized treatment plan based on your unique needs
  • Regular 50-minute sessions
  • A combination of talking therapy and practical exercises
  • Progress monitoring to ensure you're meeting your goals

Common Issues We Address

  • Anxiety and stress management
  • Depression and mood disorders
  • Self-esteem and identity issues
  • Trauma and grief
  • Life transitions and personal growth

Couples Therapy

$150 per session

Couples therapy helps partners improve their relationship by addressing communication issues, resolving conflicts, and strengthening emotional bonds. Our counselors create a supportive environment where both partners can express their needs and work toward mutual understanding.

What to Expect

  • Joint sessions with both partners
  • 60-minute sessions
  • Structured exercises and discussions
  • Homework assignments to practice new skills

Common Issues We Address

  • Communication difficulties
  • Trust and intimacy concerns
  • Conflict resolution
  • Parenting disagreements
  • Major life transitions
Book Couples Therapy

Career Counseling

$100 per session

Career counseling helps you navigate professional challenges, make informed career decisions, and achieve your work-related goals. Our career specialists provide assessments, guidance, and practical strategies to enhance your professional development.

What to Expect

  • Career assessment tools and exercises
  • 50-minute sessions
  • Concrete action plans
  • Resources and referrals as needed

Common Issues We Address

  • Career exploration and planning
  • Job search strategies
  • Work-related stress and burnout
  • Career transitions and changes
  • Work-life balance concerns

Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about our services

Individual and career counseling sessions are typically 50 minutes long, while couples therapy sessions are 60 minutes. The initial consultation session may be slightly longer to allow time for assessment and planning.

The number of sessions varies based on your specific needs and goals. Some clients benefit from short-term counseling (6-8 sessions), while others prefer ongoing support. Your counselor will discuss recommendations during your initial consultation and regularly review progress.

Yes, confidentiality is a fundamental part of our counseling services. What you share with your counselor remains private, with a few legal exceptions related to safety concerns. These limitations will be explained during your first session.

We accept several major insurance plans and can provide documentation for out-of-network reimbursement. Please contact us to verify coverage before your first appointment.

We request 24 hours' notice for cancellations. Late cancellations or missed appointments may be subject to a fee. We understand that emergencies happen, and these situations are handled on a case-by-case basis.
